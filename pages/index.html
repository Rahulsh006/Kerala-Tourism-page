About Kerala

Kerala is a popular state located on the southwestern, Malabar Coast of India. As per the state geographical area, Kerala is the twenty second largest Indian state by area. It is surrounded by Karnataka to the north and northeast, Tamil Nadu to the east and south, and Lakshadweep Sea and Arabian Sea to the West. The state is divided into fourteen districts with the capital being Thiruvananthapuram. The local regional language of the state Malayalam is the most widely spoken language and is also the official language of the state.

Its ideal location, Kerala is fast becoming one of the top tourist destinations in the world. Breaking its long-standing identity as a slow-paced destination, Kerala Tourism repackages God’s own country as the perfect playground to acquire new and composite experiences. From high mountains and deep-cut valleys to sunny tropical beaches and placid backwaters, Kerala’s 38,863 square kilometer holds within itself a variety of landscapes that differ in settings and flavor, each one of them offering an opportunity to relish the outdoors. Several eco-friendlies, sustainable initiatives would constitute the high spot.
